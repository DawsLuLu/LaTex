	\section{CPU Rack}
	
	On the Multiview Explorer go to Configurations and Setup $>$ CPU/Expansion Racks and double click "CPU Rack".
	
	Delete any expansions modules and NX-ID5442/NX-OD5256 not needed according to the Electrical Schematics of the project.

	Use for part present sensors.
